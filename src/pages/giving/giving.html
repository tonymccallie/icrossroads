Giving

At Crossroads, we believe that giving of our first fruits is an act of worship and a way that we can honor God with what He has given us. God has greatly increased the effectiveness of Crossroads through your gifts. You have an opportunity, as at no other time in history, to transform our world through God’s Word, one life at a time.

Your tithes and gifts are vital to the everyday ministry that takes place in and through Crossroads! We ask that you would prayerfully consider how God is leading you to give.

It's secure

Online giving is actually more secure than checks or cash. With this system, you won’t have three or four different people processing your paper checks or cash. Your gift is electronically transferred directly to Crossroads’ bank and the information about your transfer is sent to Crossroads’ internal records.
