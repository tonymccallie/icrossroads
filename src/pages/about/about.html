About

Crossroads Church is committed to helping people CONNECT with God and one another, GROW to be like Jesus, and GO, serving Him by serving others.

Connect

At Crossroads we are committed to come together as a church family at our weekend services to worship God through contemporary music and to learn how to apply God’s Word, the Bible, in practical ways. It’s a great opportunity to introduce your friends and family to Jesus Christ as well. Matthew 22:37-39 “Love the Lord your God with all your heart, soul, and mind. ”This is the first and greatest commandment. And the second is like it: “Love your neighbor as yourself.”

Grow

Crossroads’ discipleship path is to simply: Connect with God and others, Grow to be like Jesus, and Go and serve. We enact this growth by attending Sunday services, participating in small groups, serving in ministry, and our spending personal time with God, reading His Word and in prayer. John 17:17 “Make them holy by your truth; teach them your word, which is truth.”

Go

At Crossroads we serve with a purpose. We do this by reaching out to the needs in our church, community, and world. We strive to GO into all the world. Matthew 28:19, 20 “…therefore, go and make disciples of all the nation.”
